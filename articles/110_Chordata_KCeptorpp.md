# Chordata: KCeptor++ advanced sensing unit

An advanced hardware sensing unit for [Chordata open source motion capture system](http://chordata.cc)

This family of boards were the evolution of the [KCeptor](109_Chordata_KCeptor.html), developed thanks to a [Kickstarter campaign](https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/chordata-motion/chordata-motion-first-open-source-motion-capture-kit/).

![KCeptor++](images/105_Chordata_kcpp.jpg)

The hardware design was comitioned to [Guido Ottaviani](https://www.guiott.com/), while I was in charge of the production supervision and Firmware development. Apart from the IMU + magnetometer sensors present in the early versions of the KCeptor, these boards featured a STM32G0 Series MCU, which allowed to unload a number of Signal processing functionalties directly on-board.

The hardware set gear was completed with a Raspberry-pi HAT, that allowed the conectivity with the KCeptors in a convenient way 

These boards were manufactured entirely within the European Union (EU), ensuring compliance with local regulations and maintaining a high standard of quality control throughout the production process.
